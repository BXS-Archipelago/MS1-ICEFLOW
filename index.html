What is iceflow?

Its like a Nordic Music Database! The Ice Flow project was created to introduce the female singers and songwriters of the Nordic Region to the rest of the world. We hope to bring you names you never heard of and to gain an insight into the talent that resides in the Nordic region


You will find a comprehensive list of artists in our gallery. However, if you know of other artists we should feature, please contact us.
